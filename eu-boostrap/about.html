Education opportunities in Europe

Since 2007, The Eu Scholarship programme for the Turkish Cypriot community has provided more than 2000 Turkish Cypriots with scholarships within the EU.

Funded by the European Union

More than 25.5 millon euro worth of scholarships have been funded by the European Union since 2007 that provided the Turkish Cypriots with opportunities to pursue their education and professional development goals in Europe.

Implemented by the Goethe-Institut

As of June 2022, the EU Scholarship Programme for the Turkish Cypriot community is being implemented by the Goethe-Institut.

ABOUT US

The EU Scholarship Programme for the Turkish Cypriot community is implemented by the Goethe-Institut. This Programme’s objective is to provide the Turkish Cypriot community with broad education opportunities within the European Union and enhance the connection with the Programme’s alumni and the EU while assisting the alumni with further professional development.

This programme is funded by the European Union and implemented by the Goethe-Institut.
